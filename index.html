Steven Erikson

Steven Erikson (born 1959) is the pseudonym of Steve Rune Lundin, a Canadian novelist, who was educated and trained as both an archaeologist and anthropologist. While at the university, he also took courses in fiction writing. His best-known work is the series, the Malazan Book of the Fallen.

Ian C. Esslemont

Ian Cameron Esslemont (born 1962) is a Canadian writer. He has a degree in Creative Writing, studied and worked as an archaeologist, travelled extensively in South East Asia, and lived in Thailand and Japan for several years. He is best-known for his series Novels of the Malazan Empire.
