RSign up

The published order of the Malazan Saga is the author's intended order and the best order in which to jump in with this series for the first time. That said, There are numerous ways that all the series of the Malazan World can be combined into a single read.

Recommendation would be; on your first read try published order, and then on re-read dive in on reading order suggested by many Malaz Fans for ultimate experience.

Steven Erikson
